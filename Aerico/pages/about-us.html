About Aerico Industries Inc.

A LEADER IN THE MILITARY COMMUNITY SINCE 1995

Aerico Industries, Inc., a professional design, and manufacturing company, has been providing various Government and commercial agencies with turn-key solutions to complex chemical monitoring and heated sample line problems since 1995.

Aerico has made significant advancements in the technology, production, and most importantly, the safety of products used to extract and destroy chemical warfare agents, and other products. Aerico is an innovative, safety-driven company, with the safety of customers and the public as our foremost goal. Extremely detailed regulations for testing are followed internally to ensure all products perform and protect.

We specialize in providing advanced and never-seen-before solutions and products.

Aerico's office and manufacturing facility is located in central Maryland, conveniently located near Aberdeen and Edgewood, Maryland, however, we serve throughout the continental U.S.

ENGINEERING

Great products start with listening to customer's needs and understanding their need. Aerico partners with its customers to engineer new solutions that are driven by safety and experience.

MANUFACTURING

Great solutions are manufactured in our own secure and safe facility. Welding. TIG. MIG. Metal fabrication. Laser technology. 3D print technology . . . and more.

CUSTOM SOLUTIONS
