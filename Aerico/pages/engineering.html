ENGINEERING

Whether you are exploring the potential use of a new technology for an old application, or a project manager responsible for implementing a state-of-the-art global environmental monitoring and control system, Aerico Industries possesses the expertise, practical experience, and most of all, the power of vision to recognize the real world solutions to tomorrow’s ever increasing challenges.

Great products start with listening to customer's needs and understanding their need. Aerico partners with you to engineer new solutions that are driven by safety and experience

Aerico Industries provides a vast array of services ranging from the allocation of basic engineering and technical resources to the implementation of large scale support programs requiring both professional and technical disciplines. The companies’ multi-talented staff possess over 41 years of cumulative experience in the areas of; Engineering, System Fabrication and Testing, Generation, Field Installation and Testing, Remedial and Preventative Field Maintenance and Logistical Support Planning and Implementation.

Aerico has been manufacturing a family of Heated Sample Transfer Lines for the chemical monitoring industry for over 24 years. Aerico’s popular HTL series sample line family has been designed specifically for high mobility field operations, capable of enduring a host of environmental extremes and customer applications. Aerico continues to enhance and extend the performance characteristics of the HTL product line in a relentless pursuit of product superiority, safety and customer satisfaction.
